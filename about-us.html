What is Adex Legends?

In this game your legend will battle with 60 other legends worldwide to claim the title of ADEX Champion! Use the variety of the legends to your strengh! Battle with several tactics to claim the crown!

GameModes

In ADEX Legends, there are 3 Gamemodes. Play ADEX is a gamemode where you can play as your legends without worrying about ranks. Ranked is where you are placed against the best to rank up and be the best. Lastly, arenas where you are placed in a 3 v 3 situation with your enemies!
